Actions

Prioritized Recommendations

  1. P1 — Critical Fix Tracking
    • Install Google Tag (gtag.js or GTM) on atlasCopco.com
    • Configure Google Ads conversion tracking (lead form, phone, chat)
    • Verify tag firing via Tag Assistant
    • Validate conversion registration in Google Ads
    • Estimated cost: 0 (if done internally) — without this, any optimization is useless
  2. P2 — High Improve Quality Score & Relevance
    • Revise RSAs to include high-volume keywords in headlines
    • Add sitelinks to all RSAs (minimum 4-6 per group)
    • Create specific landing pages per product group
    • Improve Ad Strength from Poor/Average to Good/Excellent
    • Implement call extensions and structured snippets
  3. P3 — High Adjust Bidding Strategy
    • Switch from Maximize Conversions to Maximize Clicks temporarily
    • Set max CPC (US$ 2.00-3.00) to control costs
    • After 30 conversions registered, switch to Target CPA
    • Monitor CTR and click volume impact daily
  4. P4 — Medium-High Expand & Refine Keywords
    • Pause keywords with 0 impressions in the last 90 days (~30+ keywords)
    • Add Exact Match for high-intent queries from Search Terms Report
    • Create negative keyword list ("free", "diy", "repair", "parts", "price")
    • Expand with Phrase Match on long-tail variations
    • Keep Broad Match only for discovery, controlled with negatives
  5. P5 — Medium Adjust Budget Allocation
    • Increase PAL budget if Lost IS (budget) stays > 15% (current: 19%)
    • APE loses 38% due to budget — needs MORE budget, not less
    • Allocate minimum budget for Light Towers, B-Air, and Small Towable (US$ 5-10/day each)
    • Review allocation weekly based on CTR and CPC
